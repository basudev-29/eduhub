About EduHub

Empowering students by simplifying access to essential academic resources.

Our Mission

EduHub was conceptualized with a single, clear objective: to bridge the gap between students and their academic curricula. Finding reliable syllabi, comprehensive core notes, and organized past question papers can often turn into an agonizing hunt across fragmented platforms.

We solve this hurdle by engineering a unified, cleanly categorized vault that channels curated resource repositories directly into a seamless cloud ecosystem via Google Drive.

Why Use EduHub?

Designed specifically to meet the rigorous demands of academic preparation.

Highly Structured

Resources are categorized down to specific batches, semesters, and individual core tracks (Major & Minor) so you find what you need within three clicks.

Cloud Centric

Leverages the high reliability and lightning-fast speeds of Google Drive. Read, download, or bookmark files to your cloud workspace effortlessly.

Instant Search

With an interactive, dynamic search framework on the dashboard, you can filter through 24 distinct academic disciplines instantaneously.
